Sirloin Steak with Garlic Butter Recipe

Description

Delicious Sirloin Steak with galic butter sauce. Shoot for medium rare on the steak.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 pounds beef top sirloin steaks
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat grill on high heat and lightly oil grate.
  2. Melt butter in saucepan over medium-low heat. Stir in minced garlic and garlic powder. Set aside.
  3. Season both sides of steak with salt and pepper.
  4. Place steaks on grill and cook 4 minutes per side or until medium-rare.
  5. Transfer steaks to warmed plates; brush liberally with garlic butter and allow to rest for 5 to 7 minutes.
  6. Enjoy!
